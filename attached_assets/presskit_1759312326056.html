Author Bio

Short Bio (100 words)

Chetan Gabhane is a Cloud Professional Leader, Author, and Strategic Advisor helping enterprises accelerate their cloud and AI journeys. With nearly two decades of experience across multicloud, private cloud, and automation, he has guided organizations through VMware-to-cloud transitions, sovereign cloud strategies, and AI-driven infrastructure adoption. He is the author of Reverse Engineering with Terraform and Navigating VMware Turmoil in the Broadcom Era, with an upcoming book on AI-driven private cloud. As a speaker and thought leader, Chetan shares insights on cloud modernization, enterprise resilience, and the future of technology.
